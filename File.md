## Brisket Ingredients 

- Brisket (Second Cut, with fat left on)
- Salt (Maldon or Kosher)
- Pepper
- Cloves
- Onions (chopped)
- Garlic (smashed)
- Ketchup
- Worcestershire sauce
- Honey or Agave
- Tabasco or Sriracha
- Red Wine
- Baby Carrots

[^1]: This brisket will be served with broad egg noodles, unless otherwise specified.
